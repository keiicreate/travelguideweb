Food

Explore the food options in the city!

  • Local
  • Non-local

Local Food

Chan Kee Fishball

Opening Hour: 11am-10pm

Price range: 10-20HKD

Location: Kiu Fai Mansion, King's Rd, North Point

Right next to the famous Sunbeam theatre, you can smell the special curry flavour all the way from Chan Kee. Here, enjoy your siu mai, fishball, and more cheap hong kong snacks!

Tak Hing Lung Tofu

Opening Hour: 7am-7pm

Price range: 10-20HKD

Location: 1 Marble Road, North Point

Craving for tofu? For Bao? For fresh Soybean Milk? Even Tofu Pudding? Visit Tak Hing Lung for authentic tofu goodness. It has been open for over 60 years!

Hoi Ka Sichuan Boiled Fish

Opening Hour: 12-10pm

Price range: 50-200HKD

Location: 483-487 Ground Floor shop B, King's Road, North Point

Craving for spice? Visit the best Sichuan restaurant in North Point!

Tsun Yip Street Market

Opening Hour: 6am-10pm

Price range: 50-120HKD

Location: 67 Tsun Yip Street, Kwun Tong

Tsun Yip Street Market has lots of restaurants serving affordable local food. Pay a visit if you're around!

Non-Local Food

He She Eat

Opening hour: 10am-10pm

Price range: 50-200HKD

Location: 2/F, Causeway Place, 2-10 Great George St, Causeway Bay

A really nice and affordable western food restaurant. Really nice food presentation, good for foodies!

Dragonfly Bar (18+)

Opening hour: 5pm-2am

Price range: >100HKD

Location: Shop 10 - G1, Tai Kwun, 10 Hollywood Rd, Central

*You have to be 18 years old or above

Most glamourous bar to visit, celebrate your 18 here!
