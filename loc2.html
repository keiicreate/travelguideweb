Wong Tai Sin

Wong Tai Sin Temple

Wong Tai Sin Temple

Opening Hour:

7:30am-4:30pm

Price:

Free

Address:

2 Chuk Yuen Road, Wong Tai Sin, Wong Tai Sin District, New Kowloon

How to get there:

MTR Wong Tai Sin Station, B2 exit, turn left and walk for 1 min.

About:

Wong Tai Sin Temple has a history of over 100 years. If you are looking to take some photos of a well-preserved historical place, come visit Wong Tai Si Temple! It is so beautifully built abd designed, painted with rich colours and full of culture. It is a nice getaway from our busy city, almost felt like you've time travelled to ancient China with this beautiful chinese architecture view! There's a tranquilic pond in the courtyard, wandering around makes you feel like a runaway princess who recently came back home for peace.

Besides enjoying the beauty of ancient China, Wong Tai Sin Temple is also a place you must visit if you have any wishes! It is a hotspot for people of all ages who worship god and loves fortune telling. You may find diffent god's statue scattered around the temple, and people worshipping in front drawing fortune sticks. Best of all, enjoy the smoke of incense sticks!

Always go to worship ManCheung for good grades! Oh...you like someone in your class? Go visit Yuelao aka the matchmaker, tie your red string for fate!

Going to the side of the temple, you may find two stories full of fortune telling stalls. If you need to ask for prophecy or fortune tell, make sure to pay a visit! Tips though, there will be lots of stall owners waving to you, don't go in, always follow your heart! You'll know who's the right one to tell you your fate.

Choi Hung

Choi Hung Estate

Choi Hung Estate

Opening Hour:

24hr

Price:

Free

Address:

Prince Edward Rd W, Choi Hung Estate

How to get there:

MTR Wong Choi Hung Station, C3 or C4 exit, follow the guide directions afterwards (or look up and you'll see a rainbow estate!)

About:

*Please note that this is a resident area, keep your voice low and don't disturb the neighborhood.

Want some good pics for your insta? Come visit Choi Hung Estate, the famous rainbow land. Not only the MTR station is rainbow themed, but the entire estate is painted in rainbow scheme!

Not photogenic? No problem, the Choi Hung estate is also a nice place to chill afterschool and on weekends. you may visit the court for playgrounds or play basketball! You may also see people rope-skipping or film kpop covers.

Kwun Tong

Tsun Yip Street Market

Tsun Yip Street Market

Opening Hour:

6am-10pm

Price:

50-120HKD

Address:

67 Tsun Yip Street, Kwun Tong

How to get there:

MTR Kwun Tong Station, A2 exit, (5 min walk) turn left and walk straight, turn left again when you meet Tsun Yip Lane. Keep walking and you'll see it.

About:

Kwun Tong is heart to lots of locals for food. Being the industrial area, lots of workers are crowded here, thus a lot of hidden good food.

Kwun Tong has great cheap food for students and people of all ages to enjoy. Tsun Yip Street Market has lots of restaurants serving local food. You must pay a visit if you're around!
