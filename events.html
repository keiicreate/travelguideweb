Shopping

Select 18

Opening Hour: 12-7pm

Price range: Depends on your spendings

Location: G/F, 14 Tung Street, Sheung Wan

Looking for vintage goodness? Come visit Select 18! Being one of the best vintage haul hidden gem, you may find lots of last century's goodies including posters and postcards, some fine jewelleries that gives you the old money vibes.

Apliu Street

Opening Hour: 10am-8pm

Price range: Depends on your spendings

Location: Apliu Street, Shum Shui Po

Apliu Street, also called Electronic street, is a street market full of electronic goodies. If you're looking for cheap electronics, you can find it here.

Visit

Wong Tai Sin Temple

Opening Hour: 7:30am-4:30pm

Price range: free

Location: 2 Chuk Yuen Road, Wong Tai Sin, Wong Tai Sin District, New Kowloon

Wong Tai Sin Temple is a place you must visit if you have any wishes and curious about fortune telling!

Choi Hung Estate

Opening Hour: 24 hour

Price range: free

Location: Prince Edward Rd W, Choi Hung Estate

*Please note that this is a resident area, keep your voice low and don't disturb the neighborhood.

Want some good pics for your insta? Come visit Choi Hung Estate, the famous rainbow land. Not only the MTR station is rainbow themed, but the entire estate is painted in rainbow scheme!

M+

Opening Hour: Mon-Thurs 10am-6pm, Fri to 10pm, closed on Monday

Price range: free entry, check website for admission fee

Location: M+, West Kowloon Cultural District, 38 Museum Drive, Kowloon

If you're one of the artsy peeps, M+ is your dream and home.

Victoria Harbour

Opening Hour:24 hour

Price range: free (outdoor)

Location: ictoria Harbour, Tsim Sha Tsui

Victoria Harbour is probably the most famous Hong Kong landmark. It has one of the world's top night view. Sitting by the seaside along the avenue, feeling the cool breeze and listening to the waves, this is where you find your love pounding intensely for Hong Kong.
