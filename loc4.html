Central West

Explore the great neighborhood of Central, Sai Wan and Sheung Wan area of Hong Kong island! It is a district of well-preserved British colony culture. Stepping in, you may see a lot of colony era British architectures as well as modernly designed skyscrapers, it is truly where the east meets the west, truly a view of "Pearl of the Orient".

This area could be slightly pricey for students, but you can visit lots of art galleries and small designer shops to inspire your soul! If you're looking for food, make sure to enjoy the exotic foreign food by all kinds of cultures! Central West is the heart of cultural diversion. Though it could be pricey, visiting here during weekend must be nice to hang out with your friends!

Sheung Wan

Select 18 Vintage Shop

Select 18

Opening Hour:

12pm-7pm

Price:

Depending on the items, it can range from <50HKD to>300HKD.

Address:

G/F, 14 Tung Street, Sheung Wan

How to get there:

MTR Sheung Wan Station, A2 exit, (6 min walk) turn right and walk till Morrison Street, then turn left and keep walking forward, once you cross Lok Ku Road and walk up a bit, you'll see Select 18 on your left.

About:

Looking for vintage goodness? Come visit Select 18! Being one of the best vintage haul hidden gem, you may find lots of last century's goodies including posters and postcards, some fine and nice jewelleries that gives you the old money vibes. And if you're lucky, you may even come across some great designer steals!

Stepping into the shop, you'd think you've time travelled to the last century of Hong Kong. The warm lighting and rich indoor deco is a like warm invitation to the great old golden times, make sure to appreciate the lots of handmade goodies and retro items! The owner has a passion for the 80s and 90s music, sing along to Beyond and Anita Mui's greatest hits playing in the shop!

This is such a nice place for afterschool and weekend visit, chat with the owner and you'll be surprised and immersed in his life story! Time may have washed away his youth, but his smile and love stays young, hence why the shop is looking so great!

Central

Dragonfly bar (18+)

Dragonfly Bar (18+)

Opening Hour:

5pm-2am

Price:

>100HKD

Address:

Shop 10 - G1, Tai Kwun, 10 Hollywood Rd, Central

How to get there:

MTR Central Station, D2 exit, (10 min walk) turn right and walk till Pottinger Street, then cross the road and keep walking up the stairs. Get pass Stanley Street, Wellington Street and Wyndham Street, you'll see the historical landmark Tai Kwun. Head inside, and go to G1 floor Shop 10. It is a small alley but full of surprises! If you're worried of getting lost, there will be staff around to guide you.

About:

*Please note that you have to be 18 years old to get in! Bring your id card for your visit!

For my F6 students who turns 18, you must be so stressed with DSE. Looking forward to your adulthood? Wanna celebrate your 18? You must visit Dragonfly because it is the most mesmerising bar in Hong Kong.

Don't be overwhelmed by the drink menu, assuming this could be your first visit, you may always ask the staff for recommendations! They are all very kind. Ask for something light, depending on you preferred flavour, sweet, bitter or sour, you're garunteed to get something you like. Free unlimited refill of popcorn snack bowl is provided!

Dragonfly is designed by the artist Ashley Sutton, combining European heritage with Tai Kwun aesthetics, it has a unique art nouvea style of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. The in house low lighting and soft lounge music will bring you an unforgettable cosy ambient experience.
